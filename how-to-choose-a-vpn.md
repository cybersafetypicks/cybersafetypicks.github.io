# How to Choose a VPN in 2026

## Who This Is For:
This guide is designed for non-technical users looking for clear and simple advice on choosing a Virtual Private Network (VPN).

## What To Look For:

1. **Privacy**: A good VPN will hide your internet activity from other people.
2. **Speed**: You should expect the speed to be similar or faster than using public Wi-Fi.
3. **Security**: Good security means encryption, which protects your data while it's being sent over the internet.

## Recommended Options:

- **ExpressVPN**
  - ExpressVPN is known for its fast and secure connections.
- **Surfshark**
  - Surfshark offers good privacy features without a complicated setup process.
  
## Mistakes To Avoid:
1. **Not researching thoroughly**: Make sure to read reviews and compare different options.
2. **Choosing only on price**: A cheaper VPN might not be as reliable or secure.
