# Best VPNs for Home Wi-Fi Privacy

## Who This Is For:
This guide is for non-technical users looking to secure their home Wi-Fi network privacy. 

## What To Look For:
When choosing a Virtual Private Network (VPN) for your home Wi-Fi, look for a service that offers strong encryption and good customer support.

## Recommended Options:

### 1. ExpressVPN
ExpressVPN has a no-log policy, which means it won't save any of your online activity data. It also provides fast servers around the world to ensure stable connections. They have excellent customer support available through phone and email.

### 2. Surfshark
Surfshark offers strong encryption and can route your traffic through up to five different locations at once for better privacy, especially when using public Wi-Fi. They are known for having very fast servers as well.

## Mistakes To Avoid:
- Choosing a service with weak or no encryption.
- Selecting a company that collects personal data about your online activities.

## Final Recommendation:
ExpressVPN and Surfshark both offer strong security features at good price points, but ExpressVPN is recommended due to its clear privacy policy. However, it's always important to review reviews and choose what fits best with your needs.
