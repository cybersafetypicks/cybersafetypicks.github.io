## Who This Is For
This guide is for non-technical users looking to secure their accounts and manage passwords effectively within a family setting.

## What To Look For In A Password Manager
When choosing a password manager, look for these features:
- User-friendly interface: Easy navigation and simple setup.
- Encryption: Ensures that your passwords are kept safe.
- Multi-platform support: Allows you to use it across different devices like phones, tablets, and computers.
- Security features: Adds layers of protection such as two-factor authentication.
- Parental control tools: Helps manage who can access which accounts.

## Mistakes To Avoid
- Overlooking simple features like easy setup and user-friendly interfaces.
- Not checking if the password manager supports encryption for added security.
- Ignoring parental control options to protect children from unauthorized access.
